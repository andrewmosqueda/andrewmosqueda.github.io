Paddleboarding Adventure in Dasol, Pangasinan

A peaceful paradise to unwind

Camera: Samsung Galaxy A3 2017

My photos on an unpopular beach in Dasol, Pangasinan, the owner was a retiree and he hasn't named the beach yet. Rent for shaded table is 150PHP, free parking and entrance. To wash after, you have to fetch water from a deep well and there is  a concrete toilet room to change clothes. I'll just let the photos do the talking about the beauty of the place.

I also managed to paddleboard into a river. There are no crocodiles in Pangasinan so it's safe. I took a photo from entrance up to the shallowest where my paddleboard can go.
